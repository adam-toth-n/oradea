Christmas market

The christmas market has been organized for a couple of years now, on the main square of the city. The market is open between the 6th and 31th of December

Exclusively pedestrian streets

Along with the city's main square, where the vehicle traffic is heavily restricted there is also the Republicii street, which has some of the best caffes and bistros the city has to offer. And, of course, you can continue to indulge in the city's splendid architecture, like the Rimanoczy-palace, seen in the picture.

Enjoy nature in the heart of the city

Though not nearly as busy as the some world-cities out there, from time to time it is nice to get away from the city noise while still being very much within its center. You can take some nice, long walks along the Cristul river and connect with the nature around you

Nymphea aquapark

Built in 2016 to replace the old municipal bath, this state-of-the-art aquapark has everything you would want, all year-round
