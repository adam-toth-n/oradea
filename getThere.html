How to get to Oradea

Depending on the mode of transport, there are multiple ways to get there. If you aren't coming by car, the closest and most popular airports are Budapest - Ferihegy, Satu Mare International, Cluj-Napoca International and Debrecen International; though beware that Budapest and Cluj-Napoca are both within a nearly 3 hour journey by car/bus/train from the airport.

If, however, you choose to come by car, you can follow the hungarian M3 motorway, which takes you to Oradea from the West. From either the North or the South you have the option of following the E671, and from the West the E60
